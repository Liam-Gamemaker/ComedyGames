Alphabet Word Challenge

How to Play:

  • Players take turns saying words from a given category
  • Each word must begin with a specific letter of the alphabet
  • After saying a word, press/touch that letter on the screen
  • Once a letter is used, it becomes inactive
  • The timer starts long and decreases slightly with each letter pressed
  • If time runs out, the player is eliminated
  • Players can also be manually eliminated by pressing the eliminate button
  • All remaining players win when the game ends!
  • If all letters are used, the game is completed!
